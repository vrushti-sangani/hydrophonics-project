Benefits of Solar Energy

Solar energy is a renewable and sustainable source of energy that has numerous benefits:

  • Reduces electricity bills
  • Low maintenance costs
  • Reduces carbon footprint
  • Increases energy independence
  • Creates jobs in the solar industry

Types of Solar Energy

There are two main types of solar energy:

  1. Solar photovoltaic (PV) - converts sunlight into electricity
  2. Solar thermal - uses the sun's heat to generate electricity or heat water

Uses of Solar Energy

Solar energy can be used in various applications:

  • Residential homes
  • Commercial buildings
  • Industrial processes
  • Transportation
  • Agriculture
